Step-by-Step Checkout Process (OL with Nested UL)

  1. Review items in your shopping cart.
  2. Proceed to the checkout counter.
  3. Provide payment method:
    • Credit/Debit Card
    • Cash
    • UPI/Mobile Wallet
  4. Collect your printed receipt.
  5. Bag your groceries and take them to your car.

Grocery Product Glossary (DL)

Organic
Food produced without synthetic fertilizers or pesticides.
Gluten-Free
Products that exclude a protein composite found in wheat and related grains.
Perishable
Goods that have a limited shelf life and must be refrigerated.
Bulk Bin
A section where you can buy dry goods like rice and nuts by weight.
UPC
Universal Product Code, the barcode scanned at the register.
